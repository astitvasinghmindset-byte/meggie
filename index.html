About Meggie

About Meggie Meggie is a passionate fitness and wellness coach dedicated to helping others step into their strength—physically, mentally, and emotionally. Through her work at CHS Fitness and The Works, she leads with authenticity, heart, and a deep belief that movement has the power to transform lives. Her journey is rooted in resilience, community, and a commitment to growth. Whether she’s guiding a high-energy class, sharing moments of motherhood, or showing up vulnerably in her own evolution, Meggie invites others to see change as something empowering—not overwhelming. Her mantra, “If you change the way you look at things, the things you look at change,” drives her approach to both fitness and life. Meggie’s classes and content reflect the things she values most: connection, intention, joy, and the power of showing up. She continues to inspire a community of people who are ready to move, heal, laugh, and grow—with her and with each other. If you want it to sound more formal, more casual, more motivational, or more brand-focused, I can adjust it!
